# Ecommerce & Customer Analytics Case Study

## Project Overview
This project analyzes ecommerce and customer data to uncover insights that drive **sales growth**, **cost reduction**, and **better business decisions**.
The dataset represents a fictional ecommerce company.

---

## Business Objectives

### Increase Sales
- Identify cross-sell opportunities  
- Implement upsell strategies  
- Understand customer purchasing behavior  

---

## Analysis

### Market Basket Analysis
- Identifies products frequently bought together  

---

### Geographic Analysis
- East region has higher sales than Central region  

**Key Insight:**
- Texas shows:
  - High sales  
  - High customer lifetime value  

---

### Product Insights
- Memory Foam Pet Bed = 2nd best seller   
- Identify high-performing products for promotion  

---

### Customer Insights
- High-value customers contribute most revenue  
- LTV helps identify loyal and high-priority customer segments  

---

## Key Findings
- Strong cross-selling opportunities exist due to product correlations  
- East region drives higher sales than Central region  
- Texas shows both high revenue and high customer loyalty  

---

## Recommendations

### Sales Growth
- Implement cross-sell strategies for products frequently bought together  
- Use upsell strategies by promoting higher quantities or premium products  
- Target high-LTV customers with personalized offers  

### Warehouse Strategy
- Open a new warehouse in the East region  
- Reduce shipping distance for East Coast customers   
- Enhance customer satisfaction
