## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard** developed in **Power BI** to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights.

The dashboard analyzes customer behavior, product performance, purchasing patterns, quantity distribution, shipping costs, geographic sales, and market basket relationships, enabling data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze customer distribution across different states.
- Identify high-performing products and categories.
- Measure Customer Lifetime Value (LTV).
- Evaluate shipping costs and purchase quantities.
- Discover frequently purchased product combinations.
- Support inventory optimization and cross-selling strategies.
- Deliver executive-level KPIs through interactive dashboards.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Data Clean-Up

Performed data preparation using **Power Query** before building analytical dashboards.

### Key Activities

- Cleaned **24,404 retail transaction records**
- Removed duplicates
- Validated invoice records
- Standardized product descriptions
- Prepared data model for reporting

### Business Insights

- California contains the highest number of customers.
- Several states have relatively fewer customers, indicating market expansion opportunities.
- Data validation ensured reliable downstream analytics.
- Clean, structured data improved reporting accuracy.

---

## 2️⃣ Customer Analysis

Analyzed customer distribution and lifetime value across U.S. states.

### Visuals

- Customer Distribution Map
- Customer Lifetime Value by State

### Business Insights

- California has the largest customer base.
- Customer Lifetime Value varies significantly by region.
- Geographic analysis identifies high-value markets.
- Regional insights support customer acquisition strategies.

---

## 3️⃣ Product Analysis

Evaluated product performance based on quantity sold, shipping cost, and sales contribution.

### Visuals

- Average Quantity by Product
- Average Shipping Cost by Product
- Sales by Category Treemap

### Business Insights

- **Food** generated the highest total sales.
- **Disposables** ranked second in sales contribution.
- **Shake Perfect Portions Plus** recorded the highest average purchase quantity.
- **Taste of the Wild High Prairie Grain-Free Dry Dog Food** incurred the highest average shipping cost.
- A small group of products contributes a significant portion of total revenue.

---

## 4️⃣ Quantity Analysis

Examined purchasing behavior based on order quantity.

### Business Insights

- Most purchases consist of **1–5 units**.
- Quantity **1** represents the highest percentage of transactions.
- Revenue decreases as purchase quantity increases.
- Bulk purchases occur less frequently.
- Small, recurring purchases drive overall sales.

---

## 5️⃣ Executive Summary

Provides a consolidated business overview for decision-makers.

### Dashboard Includes

- Total Sales by State
- Total Sales by Product
- Product Category Performance

### Business Insights

- California generated the highest total sales.
- Food products dominate overall revenue.
- Top-selling products account for a large percentage of sales.
- Geographic analysis highlights high-performing sales regions.
- Executive KPIs support strategic decision-making.

---

## 6️⃣ Market Basket Analysis

Analyzed purchasing patterns to identify products commonly bought by customers.

### Business Insights

- Pet essentials dominate customer purchases.
- Frequently purchased products include:
  - Earth Rated Dog Poop Bags
  - Dog & Puppy Pads
  - Pet Grooming Brush
  - Memory Foam Pet Beds
  - Cat Food
- Product affinity analysis reveals strong cross-selling opportunities.
- Frequently purchased combinations can improve promotional strategies.
